Ugh... dress codes?

Mehndi

Go Green - Ethnic Indian Wear
Hey lovely people! For our Mehndi celebration, we’re bringing in the vibrant vibes of nature. "Go Green" is the dress code, so let’s turn the event into a gorgeous sea of green with your ethnic Indian wear!
Think emerald sarees, jade kurtas, lime lehengas, or anything that screams green and festive! Let’s dazzle together and make this celebration as colorful and lively as the love we’re celebrating!

Sangeet Night

Let’s Go Shimmery Shimmery
Hey party people! The Sangeet Night is where the magic happens—8 PM to 12 AM, we’re turning up the music and the sparkle! The dress code? "Let’s go Shimmery Shimmery!"
Bring out your sequins, glitter, metallics, and anything that blings! Whether it’s a dazzling saree, a shimmering kurta, or a gown that could light up the dance floor, it’s your time to shine—literally!
Let’s make the night unforgettable with music, moves, and a whole lot of sparkle! 🌟💃🕺
Can’t wait to see you all dazzle! 🎶✨

Haldi - Yellow Time

The Haldi ceremony is all about joy, laughter, and blessings, so let’s match the vibe with the happiest color of all—YELLOW!
Channel your inner sunshine with gorgeous yellow attire. Think vibrant sarees, flowy kurtas, chic lehengas, or anything that radiates those bright Haldi-day vibes!
Let’s turn this day into a glowing celebration of love, laughter, and golden hues! 🌼✨

Wedding

Let’s Go All Indian
It’s time for the big day—the Wedding Ceremony! This is your moment to dazzle in your finest Indian attire and celebrate the richness of our traditions in style.
Think regal sarees, majestic sherwanis, glamorous lehengas, or dashing kurtas! Let’s create a kaleidoscope of elegance and color as we come together to celebrate love and happiness.
Bring your A-game, your best twirls, and your festive spirit—it’s a wedding to remember, and we can’t wait to celebrate it with all of you! 💃🕺🎉
P.S. Don’t forget to bring your biggest smiles and best moves for the dance floor! 🎶✨
